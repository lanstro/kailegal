Property

Get your legal advice, market insights and practical know-how in one stop.

We live and breathe property. Come to Kai Legal for expert guidance through the entire property development process: pre-purchase due diligence, setting up the acquisition company or trust, the purchase itself, subdivision, leasing, permit application, engaging builders and sale.

We will be there to spot and clear the common roadblocks like objections at council, dealing with encumberances on the land, and VCAT appeals.

Of course, if you only need a simple conveyance, we are more than happy to assist. Just give us a call before you make your offer, for a free review of the section 32 vendor's statement!

Tax

There are many ways to hold your assets and investments, each with subtly different tax consequences. It is a minefield out there, and we do the hard work and planning to safely guide you through it without triggering any undesirable tax consequences in the future.

Alternatively, if you are facing a tax audit or duty investigation, or have received an assessment that is too high, we can use our years of experience at the Victorian State Revenue Office and Australian Tax Office to help you achieve the best outcome.

Competition & Consumer

Top tier competition law advice at small firm rates.

With our deep competition law experience, we can help you with any competition or consumer law issue, big or small. Whether it be the ACCC, a competitor, supplier or customer giving you grief, Kai Legal can help. We can help with a wide range of issues, such as checking advertising for accuracy, reviewing your consumer agreements and returns policies, rolling out compliance training and filing notifications with the ACCC.

Equally, if you are a consumer and are having trouble with getting a refund from a supplier, or have other complaints you would like to pursue, we can help.
